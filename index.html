Web Audio Technologist


So what the heck is a web audio technologist? I like to think that it's a developer whose focus is to use audio to make the web a cooler, more inclusive place. I might be coding utilities for Amazon's Alexa, Google Home, or other voice-activated products. I could also be developing new accessibility tools to help visually impaired people enjoy the internet. I might also be creating new online synthesizers and step sequencers for musicians to collaborate with their friends. Long story short, I look for ways to put my background in music/audio and the web together to make people's lives better.
