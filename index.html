Web Audio Technologist


So what the heck is a web audio technologist?

A Web Audio Technologist is a developer who uses audio to make the web a cooler, more inclusive place. I might be creating utilities for smart speaker appliances or creating new accessibility features for websites. I might also be creating new online synthesizers and step sequencers for musicians to collaborate with friends.

Long story short: I look for ways to put my background in music/audio and the web together to make people's lives better.
